Project 01

AI Agent for Government E-Seva Services

AI / Public Services

A voice-first, multilingual AI agent automating government certificate applications end-to-end — DigiLocker document fetching to legally-valid eSign via OTP. Reduces a 7–15 day process to 15 minutes, across 22 Indian languages.

The Problem

  • 3–5 physical visits per application; 7–15 days to process
  • 35% rejection rate — errors and incomplete forms
  • English-only portals with no service guidance
  • ₹300/visit × 3 visits = lost daily wages for rural citizens

The Solution

  • Zero visits — fully online, 15-minute process
  • 22 Indian languages + voice support via Bhashini API
  • DigiLocker auto-fetch + legally valid eSign via OTP
  • AI recommends exact service & validates documents in real-time

Project 02

Foody — National Surplus Food Redistribution Platform

AI / Social Impact

An AI-enabled platform connecting hotels, consumers, and NGOs to redistribute surplus food safely. Real-time risk scoring, automated expiry-to-NGO transfer, and OCR-based food safety verification — reducing food wastage at scale.

How It Works

  • Hotel uploads food with quantity, expiry, price
  • Consumers order — inventory reduces in real-time
  • Automated check every minute detects near-expiry food
  • Unsold food auto-transferred to nearest NGO for pickup

Key Features

  • Real-time risk scoring engine for food safety
  • OCR-based document verification for food batches
  • OpenStreetMap integration for NGO location matching

Project 03

AI-Powered Food Demand Forecasting System

AI / SDG

ML-based decision support platform predicting daily food demand for college hostels using historical data and contextual signals. Gives mess administrators a clean dashboard with actionable prep insights — no ML expertise required.

The Problem

  • College hostels prepare food based entirely on guesswork
  • No data-driven system to predict actual demand
  • Excess food causes financial losses and environmental waste

What It Does

  • Scikit-Learn models trained on historical consumption data
  • Contextual signals: day of week, events, holidays
  • React + Tailwind dashboard with daily prep recommendations
  • Tracks consumption trends and wastage over time
